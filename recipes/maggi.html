Masala Maggi

A recipe we all grew up with, but never grown out of. Enjoy the nostalgia

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 large Onion, chopped
  • ½ tsp Degi red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 large Tomato, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ cup Green peas
  • ¼ cup Sweet corn kernels, optional
  • 2 ½ cups Water
  • 2 pkt Instant noodles
  • 1 ½ tbsp Instant noodle masala
  • 2 no. Green chillies (less spicy & chopped)
  • ½ inch Ginger (peeled & finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Butter, cubed
  • 1 tbsp Coriander leaves, finely chopped

Process

  1. In a kadai or sauce pot, add oil, once it's hot, add onion and saute it well until translucent.
  2. Add degi red chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder and saute it for a minute.
  3. Add tomato, salt to taste and cook it well, add green peas, sweet corn kernels, water and let it cook for 7-8 minutes on medium flame.
  4. Add instant noodles, cover it with the lid and cook it for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add instant noodle masala, green chillies, ginger and mix all well.
  6. Finish it with some butter and coriander leaves.
  7. Transfer it to a serving dish and garnish it with coriander sprig.
  8. Serve hot.
