Tomato Soup

TOMATO SOUP with GARLIC BREAD - One of the most popular soup recipes, especially when ordering in a restaurant, Tomato soup is never out of trend.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 6-7 no. Garlic cloves
  • 1 medium Onion, sliced
  • 2 medium Carrots (peeled & roughly chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp Sugar
  • 10-12 medium Tomatoes, cut into half
  • 4-5 cups Water
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp Degi red chilli powder
  • 2-3 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil (for finishing)

Process

  1. In a sauce pot, heat oil, add garlic, onion, carrots and saute it for 2-3 minutes until it's translucent.
  2. Add salt to taste, sugar and saute it well.
  3. Add tomato and saute it for a minute. Add water, salt to taste and let it simmer.
  4. Cover it with the lid and cook it for 10-15 minutes on low to medium flame.
  5. Add degi red chili powder and grind it with the help of a hand blender.
  6. Strain the soup and transfer it to a bowl.
  7. In the same sauce pot, add extra virgin olive oil, pour the prepared tomato soup and let it simmer for a while.
  8. In a serving dish or bowl, add oregano, chili flakes, pour the prepared tomato soup, top it with some prepared basil pesto and garnish it with basil leaves.
  9. Serve hot with prepared garlic bread.
